# ADR-005: Layered Architecture

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Embedding business logic inside route handlers leads to tight coupling.

## Decision
Adopt a layered structure:
- Routes (HTTP layer)
- Services (business logic)
- Data access layer (database queries)

## Consequences

### Positive
- Testability
- Maintainability
- Clear separation of concerns

### Negative
- Increased structural complexity
